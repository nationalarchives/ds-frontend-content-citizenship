Rights of 'coloured' people
This document is a resolution sent by a public meeting of 'coloured' people in South Africa to Lord Milner, the British High Commissioner. The resolution protests at laws and measures 'enimical to our welfare... calculated to harass and annoy us without cause'. Such measures were the forerunners of the official policy of apartheid introduced in South Africa in 1948.
Catalogue reference: DO 119/593 no.3801/1 (c. August 1901)

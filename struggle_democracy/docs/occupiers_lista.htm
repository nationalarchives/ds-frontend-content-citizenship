Widening of New Street (list of occupiers and tenants), 1861

This is a list of occupiers and tenants affected by the proposed widening of New Street at the junction with Worcester Street (see Birmingham redevelopment plan above) . A similar list of occupiers was produced when the area was altered again in 1946.

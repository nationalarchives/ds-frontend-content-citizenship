National Land Company register, 1847
Lovell's name appears halfway down the list in this extract from the register of subscribers to the National Land Company. The company was set up to help ordinary people buy a house. You can tell from their occupations that without such help the subscribers would not have had the means to buy a home of their own.
Catalogue reference: BT 41/474/2659, Part 1 (May 1847)

Reform League poster, 1867
PRO HO 45/7854/29, no. 68

THE
Reform League.
IMPORTANT NOTICE.
To the Members of the Reform League Branches and other Reform Associations, and to Trades' Societies, Temperance Societies, and other organised bodies of Working Men.
Hold yourselves in readiness for the Great National Reform Demonstration, under the auspices of the Reform League to be held in London on the Monday after the opening of Parliament.
Provincial Reformers are earnestly requested to arrange for special Trains to run to London on that occasion.
Branches are desired to prepare their own Banners and Flags. The League colours are blue, red, and green - to represent the National Colours of England, Scotland, and Ireland.
FOR PRICE OF FLAGS APPLY TO THE SECRETARY.
By order of the Council,
GEORGE HOWELL, Sec.
8, Adelphi Terrace, Strand, W.C.
N.B. - Working Men, make your arrangements early.
For further information, apply to the Secretary, as above.
Secretaries of Societies are requested to distribute these Bills amongst their members.
Note. - Persons wishing to contribute articles for the Reform League Bazaar, are requested to communicate at once with the Secretary.
N.B. - Parliament is to be opened on Tuesday, February 5th, the League Demonstration will therefore take place on Monday, the 11th day of February.
WORKING MEN, PREPARE.

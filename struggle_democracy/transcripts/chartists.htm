Chartist demonstration in Birmingham, 1848
PRO HO 45/2410, Part 3, no. P599
LIBERTY
POLITICAL EQUALITY,
FRATERNITY!
"THE CHARTER, AND NO SURRENDER."
The Inhabitants of the Town and District of Birmingham are respectfully informed, that a
DEMONSTRATION
OF THE
FRIENDS OF CHARTISM
WILL TAKE PLACE ON A
PIECE of GROUND,
NEAR THE PEOPLE'S HALL, ON
Sunday Next, June 11, 1848,
WHEN MR.
SAMUEL KYDD,
OF SCOTLAND,
Will attend and Address the Meeting for the purpose of shewing the CITIZENS of ENGLAND that we will neither be Tyranised over, Starved nor Deceived by WHIG REFORM any longer.
CHAIR TO BE TAKEN AT THREE O'CLOCK.
By Order of the Council,
J. NISBETT, Chairman.
R. B. MOODY & Co., Printers, 12, Cannon Street, Birmingham.

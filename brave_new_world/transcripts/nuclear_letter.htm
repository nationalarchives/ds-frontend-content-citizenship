'Re H Bomb Tests' (protest letter)
Catalogue reference: FO 371/129238
 
46. Bristol Road
   
Ipswich.
 
14/3/57

To the Rt. Hon. Selwyn-Lloyd M.P.

Re H. Bomb Tests
Dear Sir /

I wish to protest very strongly against any form of bomb testing because:-
1. Highest scientific authorities have proved their ever increasing dangers.
2. This race for power can lead to nothing but world destruction.
3. Millions in money are being wasted, while millions of human beings are starving.
I plead your good sense in consulting with our allies and the U.S.S.R. before further harm is done.

Yours sincerely
Ernest M. Starr

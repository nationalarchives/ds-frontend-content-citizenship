THE SEVEN RULES OF HEALTH
You and your family will stand a better chance of avoiding colds, influenza and other common ailments - as well as more serious diseases such as tuberculosis - if you follow these simple rules. The rules offer the best guidance on how to improve your health and increase your vitality.

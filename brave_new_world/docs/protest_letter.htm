'Re H Bomb Tests' (protest letter)
Like the one from Southend and District Peace Council reproduced above, this letter to Foreign Secretary Selwyn-Lloyd concisely protests at the folly of nuclear testing. A partial test ban treaty was eventually signed between the UK, USA and USSR in 1963.
Catalogue reference: FO 371/129238 (14 March 1957)

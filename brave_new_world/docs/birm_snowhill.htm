Improvements to Snow Hill Wharf, 1946
Part of a plan showing proposed improvements to the Snow Hill Wharf area of Birmingham, produced for the Birmingham Corporation Bill of 1946.

Also reproduced here is a list indicating how many 'persons of the working class' resided as tenants or lodgers in each house for which purchase was sought in furtherance of the Snow Hill Wharf scheme. This list was annexed to the Birmingham Corporation Plan, and the houses have numbers corresponding to those tinted pink on the plan.
HLRO Deposited Plan, H.L., 1945-1946, B3, Birmingham Corporation

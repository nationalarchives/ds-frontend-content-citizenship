Occupiers and owners of premises in Worcester Street and New Street, 1946

Book of reference listing each property on Worcester Street and New Street in Birmingham affected by the proposed improvements, and listing the names of the owners, lessees and occupiers. This book of reference was annexed to the Birmingham Corporation Plan, and the properties have numbers that correspond to those on the plan. Previously, this area had been affected by the redevelopment plan of 1861.
HLRO Deposited Plan, H.L., 1945-1946, B3, Birmingham Corporation

'Bill of Rights' for Northern Ireland
This document considers a request from the Northern Ireland Civil Rights Association for a 'Bill of Rights' to protect the Catholic minority in Northern Ireland. Catholics had long been discriminated against by the Protestant-dominated political system, for things like local housing and jobs. They compared their position to that of 'coloured' people in Britain.
Catalogue reference: CJ 4/13 (1970)

Creation of Citizens' Advice Bureaux (press release)

Citizens' Advice Bureaux were started in 1939 to help people cope with the problems they would face during the war. However, once the war was over, they continued to act as a voluntary service, giving advice on consumer affairs and legal matters.
Catalogue reference: AST 7/420 (30 August 1939)

Courtesy of NCVO, formerly the National Council of Social Service

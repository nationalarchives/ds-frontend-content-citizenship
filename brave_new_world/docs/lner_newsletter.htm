LNER newsletter, 1926

This special newsletter was printed by the London and North Eastern Railway Company to keep the public informed and promote a feeling of 'business as usual' during the General Strike of May 1926. The company, in common with other transport organisations, mobilised the support of volunteers to keep a skeleton service running.
Catalogue reference: RAIL 393/321 (13 May 1926)

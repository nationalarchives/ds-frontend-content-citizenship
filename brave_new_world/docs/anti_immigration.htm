Anti-immigration flyers
These two leaflets illustrate the backlash from white groups against postwar immigration. They blame the government for encouraging immigration, and call for foreigners to be sent back home. They also claim that foreigners are taking jobs from British workers - despite the fact that immigration was initially encouraged due to a labour shortage in Britain.
Catalogue reference: HO 325/9 (c.1959-61)

Improvements to Worcester Street and New Street, 1946

Part of a plan showing proposed improvements to Worcester Street and New Street, produced for the Birmingham Corporation Bill of 1946. The properties have numbers that correspond to the numbers in the book of reference, listing occupiers and owners, produced to accompany the plan. This area had previously been affected by the redevelopment plan of 1861.
HLRO Deposited Plan, H.L., 1945-1946, B3, Birmingham Corporation

Catalogue reference: SP 14/103, no. 42 (I)
Witnesseth that I, Owyn Evans of the Court of England have received of William Michell of Ottery 10 s[hillings] of English money for freeing of the parish of Ottery from the commission of pressing maidens for the Barmudos and Virginia, in witness whereof I have given a discharge and hath hereunto sett my hand the day and year abovesaid.

The mark of Owin Evans.
